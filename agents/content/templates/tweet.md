## Character Limits

- **Standard tweet:** 280 characters
- **Aim for:** Under 200 (more readable, more RT-able)
- **With image:** Text can be shorter, image does the work

---

## Formatting Rules

1. One idea per tweet
2. Line breaks for emphasis (but don't overdo)
3. No hashtags unless truly relevant (#buildinpublic is fine)
4. Emojis sparingly (0-2 max)
5. Links at the end, not middle
